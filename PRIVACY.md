# Privacy Policy for Cloudcostify

**Effective Date:** May 28, 2026

At Cloudcostify, we care about developer privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the Cloudcostify CLI, our API, and our services.

## 1. Data Processed by the CLI and API
The Cloudcostify CLI and backend API are designed to analyze infrastructure configuration files (such as Pulumi preview JSON) to estimate cloud costs. 

* **No Personal Data:** The CLI and cost-estimation API do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data (such as names, IP addresses, or cloud credentials).
* **Infrastructure Metadata:** The infrastructure metadata sent to our API is processed in real-time solely to calculate your cost estimate and is not saved or stored on our servers.

## 2. Data Collected via Third-Party Authentication (Clerk)
When you sign up for the Cloudcostify Beta or create an account, we use **Clerk** as our third-party identity and authentication provider. Through Clerk, we access limited personal data to manage your account:

* **Information Collected:** Email address, user ID, and account creation timestamps.
* **Purpose:** To manage your beta access, verify your identity, and communicate important updates about Cloudcostify.
* **Data Processor:** This data is securely stored and handled by Clerk in accordance with their privacy standards.

## 3. GitHub Marketplace Data
If you install Cloudcostify via GitHub Marketplace, GitHub may share basic installation metadata with us (such as your GitHub organization or username) to facilitate the installation. We do not use this data for marketing or tracking.

## 4. Third-Party Data Processors
We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. We only use trusted third-party services necessary to run Cloudcostify:
* **Clerk:** For user authentication and account management.
* **Railway:** For hosting our secure, stateless cost-estimation API.

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)
Depending on your location, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of the personal data we hold about you (which is primarily your email address in Clerk). 

If you wish to delete your Cloudcostify account and wipe your data, please contact us.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out via our website:
